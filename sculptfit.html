Project Vision

To empower gym-goers, whether beginners or enthusiasts, by providing a seamless, weight-lifting app experience that facilitates personal growth. SculptFit will help users easily track workouts, monitor nutritional information, and maintain a healthier lifestyle.

Challenges

  • Beginners often feel overwhelmed by tracking and workout complexity.
  • Requirement for a clean interface without clutter.
  • Providing variety and progress for all user levels.
  • Ensuring the app remains user-friendly at all times.

Kickoff

For this personal branding project, I utilized a goal-centered design approach to define the core objectives. I conducted thorough research, user interviews, and competitive analysis to find the best solutions for lifting and wellness.

Meet the Users

Name: Jake

Age: 23

Occupation: Tech Support

Jake is new to the gym and often feels overwhelmed by all the equipment and workout options. He enjoys learning about fitness routines and loves trying pre-made workouts that guide him step by step. The app helps him stay consistent and motivated while exploring different exercises without feeling lost.

Name: Blaire

Age: 21

Occupation: University Student

Blaire is interested in fitness and enjoys customizing her workouts to match her specific goals. She wants an app that allows her to plan her schedule, select exercises freely, and see her progress visually. She values flexibility but also wants a clean, intuitive design that fits into her busy academic life.

Name: Meera

Age: 32

Occupation: Software Engineer

Meera is a busy professional who struggles to find consistent time for workouts. She prefers structured routines and quick, effective workouts she can fit into her schedule. The app helps her track streaks, monitor progress, and stay motivated without having to spend too much time planning.
