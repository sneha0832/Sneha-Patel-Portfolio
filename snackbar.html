Project Vision

To make ordering food at the theatre fast, convenient, and enjoyable by providing users with an intuitive app that allows them to browse menus, place orders, and receive notifications, minimizing wait times and enhancing the theatre experience.

Challenges

  • Users get frustrated waiting in long queues during intermission.
  • Menus at the theatre are hard to navigate quickly.
  • Users want real-time updates about order status.
  • Designing an app that is simple enough for first-time users but efficient for frequent theatre-goers.

Kickoff

For this personal branding project, I utilized a goal-centered design approach to define the core objectives. I conducted thorough research, user interviews, and competitive analysis to find the best solutions for theatre-goers.
