Project Vision

To enhance the University of Tampa student app by implementing accessibility features that ensure all students, including those with visual, auditory, or motor impairments, can navigate the app, access essential campus information, and have an inclusive digital experience.

Challenges

  • Existing app lacked accessibility features for users with disabilities.
  • Users with visual, auditory, or motor impairments could not easily access key functions like maps, dining, and parking.
  • Balancing accessibility enhancements without disrupting the existing interface..
  • Ensuring compliance with accessibility standards like WCAG.

Kickoff

For this independent research project, I focused on identifying gaps in the University of Tampa app related to accessibility. I began with:

Reviewing the existing app features and flows.
Researching best practices in inclusive mobile design
Who on campus is most affected by accessibility barriers?
Conducting literature review on accessibility guidelines (WCAG)
Which features are most critical for an inclusive experience?
What changes can improve usability without overwhelming existing users?

Meet the Users

Name: Alex
